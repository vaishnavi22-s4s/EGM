About Us
Driving Change for a Greener Tomorrow
Eden Green Mobility (EGM) is an initiative by Eden Green Energy Private Limited, headquartered in Tamil Nadu. We specialize in eco-friendly shared mobility services, empowering all stakeholders in the EV ecosystem while promoting a cleaner, greener future.
Our Mission
Transforming transportation by ensuring EV adoption is smooth and within reach.
Our Vision
Creating a greener future through advanced mobility solutions and shared progress.
